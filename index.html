Episode 0.9.4: Symfony2, frameworks and cross platform mobile app development

August 5, 2011

Carey, Gav, Sean and Codes talking shop

After last week’s culture heavy podcast, this week we got a bit more into the tech! We’ve compressed the audio a little more this time to make the file size smaller. This week, Gav and Carey were joined by recurring guest Big Sean, as well as coder Craig “Codes” Marvelley. We chatted over a pint about:

  • Symfony2 framework (and frameworks in general)
  • Playing with Bash On Balls
  • The importance of input devices
  • Is Blackberry the IE6 of the mobile market?
  • iOs development and cross platform mobile dev
  • What Apple have done right with iOs, and what are the tradeoffs
  • The importance of GNU-style pure open source software
